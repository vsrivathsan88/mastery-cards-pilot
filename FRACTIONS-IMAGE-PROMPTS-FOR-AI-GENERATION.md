### Neo-Brutalist Design Rules
**MUST FOLLOW:**
1. **Thick borders:** 4px solid #1A1D2E on ALL shapes
2. **Offset shadows:** 6px 6px 0 #1A1D2E (NO blur, hard shadows only)
3. **Rounded corners:** 12-20px border-radius on rectangles/cards
4. **Flat design:** NO gradients, NO soft shadows, NO 3D effects
5. **High contrast:** All text/shapes must meet WCAG AA standards
6. **Bold shapes:** Clean, tactile, geometric precision

### Typography Standards
- **Font:** -apple-system, SF Pro Rounded, system-ui, or similar sans-serif
- **Weights:** 700 (bold) or 800 (extra bold) ONLY
- **Text color:** #1A1D2E (dark navy) always
- **Sizes:** Minimum 14px (prefer 16-24px for readability)
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned, horizontal only

### Mathematical Clarity Principles
**Critical for educational integrity:**
1. **Geometric precision:** Equal parts must be EXACTLY equal (use protractors, rulers)
2. **No leading hints:** Show mathematical structures WITHOUT annotations
3. **Observable differences:** Unequal parts must be OBVIOUSLY unequal (30%+ variance)
4. **Clean negative space:** No clutter, focus on mathematical relationships
5. **Invitation to explore:** Visual design invites questioning, not conclusion-giving

### Accessibility Requirements
- **Contrast ratio:** Minimum 4.5:1 for text (WCAG AA)
- **No color-only info:** Use shapes, borders, and patterns alongside color
- **Readable fonts:** Minimum 14px, bold weights for clarity
- **Alt text:** Provided separately in pedagogy mapping files
- **Clear shapes:** 4px borders ensure visibility for low-vision users
