## General Production Notes

### File Format
- Primary: SVG (vector, scalable)
- Alternative: PNG at 2x resolution for crisp display
- Color space: sRGB

### Typography
- Sans-serif fonts: Arial, Helvetica, or similar clean fonts
- Font weights: Regular (400), Bold (700)
- Text always horizontal, left-aligned unless specified

### Color Palette (Consistent Across All Images)
- Primary outline: #333333 (dark gray)
- Division lines: #666666 (medium gray)
- Correct indicator: #E8F5E9 (light green)
- Incorrect indicator: #FFEBEE (light red)
- Blue accent: #2196F3
- Green accent: #4CAF50
- Purple accent: #7B1FA2
- Orange accent: #FF6F00
- Background: #FFFFFF (pure white)

### Accessibility
- High contrast (WCAG AA minimum)
- No color-only information (use shapes/labels too)
- Clear readable fonts minimum 10px
- Alternative text provided in pedagogy mapping

### Quality Standards
- Clean geometric precision
- No anti-aliasing artifacts
- Crisp edges and lines
- Professional educational quality
- Age-appropriate for grade 3 (8-9 years old)
